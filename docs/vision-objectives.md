## Product 1: Career Guidance System

### Target Users
- **Students** (Class 6-12) - Primary users
- **Parents** - Supporting their children
- **Counselors** - Guiding students
- **Schools** - Tracking student outcomes

### What It Does

```
DISCOVER вҶ’ EXPLORE вҶ’ PLAN вҶ’ ACHIEVE вҶ’ TRANSITION
```

#### 1. DISCOVER (Public - No Login)
- Take career assessments (RIASEC, MBTI, DISC, SPARK)
- Get instant personality insights
- Discover matching careers
- **Goal:** "Aha! I didn't know I was suited for this!"

#### 2. EXPLORE (Public вҶ’ Logged In)
- Browse detailed career profiles
- Learn about RUB colleges
- Find scholarships
- Talk to counselors
- **Goal:** "This career fits me perfectly!"

#### 3. PLAN (Logged In - Student)
- Create personalized career plan
- Set academic goals
- Choose subjects (Class 11-12)
- Track progress
- **Goal:** "I know what I need to do to get there"

#### 4. ACHIEVE (Tracked by School)
- Homework contributes to skills
- Attendance matters
- Results monitored
- Teacher feedback
- **Goal:** "I'm on track for my dream college!"

#### 5. TRANSITION (Class 12 вҶ’ Beyond)
- BCSE results integration
- Scholarship applications
- RUB college applications
- Alumni network
- **Goal:** "I made it! рҹҺү"

## Why This Works

### For Students
- **Free career assessments** - No barrier to entry
- **Personalized insights** - Based on their personality
- **Clear path forward** - Step-by-step guidance
- **College connections** - Direct links to RUB, scholarships

### For Schools
- **Student attraction** - "We have career counseling!"
- **Data-driven decisions** - See student interests/trends
- **Better outcomes** - Students are more engaged
- **Ministry compliance** - Reports ready for BCSE/RUB

### For Counselors
- **Tools at scale** - Manage hundreds of students
- **Data-backed insights** - Assessment results + grades
- **Progress tracking** - See student growth over time
- **Resource library** - Curated content for students

## Key Differentiator

Most school management systems are just **admin tools**.

Career Compass is:
- **Admin tool** (what schools need)
- **PLUS career guidance** (what students want)
- **Connected ecosystem** (data flows both ways)

**Result:** Schools buy it because they have to вҶ’ Students love it because they want to.
