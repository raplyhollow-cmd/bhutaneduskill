RBAC Logic

Permissions are mapped via the user_roles and role_permissions tables. This ensures a Teacher cannot view Ministry Heatmaps, and Parents can only see data for students linked to their CID.

Relational Integrity Logic

Utilizing **Neon Serverless Postgres**, we implemented a tenant-first indexing strategy. High-query columns like clerkUserId and schoolId are indexed to ensure sub-100ms response times despite the massive table count.

5. AI Core (Gemini 1.5 Pro)

The "Sentinel" & "Coach" Models

Our AI core is role-aware. For **Students**, it acts as a Career Coach, analyzing assessment results against Royal University of Bhutan (RUB) scholarship criteria. For **Ministry Officials**, it acts as a Policy Advisor, synthesizing regional performance data into actionable briefings.
