## 6. Parental Consent for Minors

### 6.1 Special Protection for Children

The Platform is designed for students in Classes 6-12 (ages approximately 11-18). We provide additional protection for minors:

**For students under 18:**
- Parental consent is required before account creation
- Parents/guardians can access their child's educational records
- Limited data collection to what is educationally necessary
- No marketing to students

**For students under 13:**
- Verifiable parental consent required before data collection
- Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data
- No behavioral advertising

### 6.2 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:
- Review their child's personal information
- Request deletion of their child's data
- Opt-out of certain data processing
- Access educational records

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### 8.1 Technical Security

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 for stored data
- **Authentication:** OAuth 2.0 via Clerk
- **Authorization:** Role-based access control
- **Audit Logging:** Comprehensive activity tracking

### 8.2 Organizational Security

- Access controls and background checks
- Regular security training
- Incident response procedures
- Data protection impact assessments

### 8.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Rationale |
