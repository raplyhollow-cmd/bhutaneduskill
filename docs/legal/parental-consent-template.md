## Section 5: Data Security

We protect your child's information with:

- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access for school staff
- **Audit Logging:** All access to student records is logged
- **Secure Authentication:** Password-protected accounts
- **Regular Security Reviews:** Ongoing security assessments

---

## Section 6: Your Rights as a Parent

You have the right to:

### 6.1 Access
- View all information we hold about your child
- Request a copy of your child's records

### 6.2 Correction
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Update contact details

### 6.3 Deletion
- Request deletion of your child's account (subject to school record retention policies)
- Withdraw consent at any time

### 6.4 Portability
- Request your child's data in a readable format
- Transfer records to another school

### 6.5 Restriction
- Limit certain types of processing
- Opt-out of non-essential features
