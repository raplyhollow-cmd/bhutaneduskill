## Getting Started

### Account Creation

1. **Sign Up**
   - Visit your school's Bhutan EduSkill URL
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
   - Enter your school code (provided by your school administrator)
   - Choose your role: Student, Teacher, Parent, Counselor, or Admin

2. **Verification**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - Set up your profile with required information

3. **Login**
   - Go to your school's Bhutan EduSkill URL
   - Enter your email and password
   - You will be redirected to your portal dashboard

### Portal Navigation

Each portal has a consistent navigation structure:

- **Dashboard** - Overview of key metrics and recent activities
- **Profile** - Manage your personal information
- **Settings** - Configure preferences and notifications
- **Help** - Access documentation and support
